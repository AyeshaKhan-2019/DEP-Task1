Practice Areas

CORPORATE LAW

We provide services in formation, dissolution, and all other legal aspects of the administration of corporations.

FAMILY LAW

Our firm specializes in handling cases related to child welfare, civil unions, domestic partnerships, marriages and divorce.

ENVIRONMENTAL LAW

We have dealt with cases involving natural resource management, the curbing of pollution, or disputes about land and littoral use.

HEALTH LAW

Health law is focuses on legislation related to health care. We have represented a varied spectrum of clients from health care to pharmaceutical industries.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY LAW

Our lawyers have strong technical backgrounds and extensive legal experience in handling Intellectual Property cases to protect the rights of inventors, authors, and businesses to their tangible and intangible creations.

CIVIL RIGHTS LAW

Our firm has work on cases involving discrimination and unfair practices that infringe on human rights and liberties such as expression, employment, housing, education, or other entitlements.

IMMIGRATION LAW

Our lawyers work with individual clients at all stages of the naturalization process.

REAL STATE LAW

Our attorneys have experience in working on residential or commercial transactions with cases related to land or construction ownership, development, litigation, tenant rights, or landlord disputes.

LABOR LAW

Labor law is concerned with the relations between workers and their employers on matters ranging from wages and compensation, through harassment, to discrimination based on gender, disability, or age.

TAX LAW

Tax law is a dynamic field that deals with domestic and international transactions. Because of the frequent modifications to local, state, and federal codes and the complexity of fiscal policy that guides these changes, our experts are always up-to-date to guide you.

CRIMINAL LAW

Criminal law focuses on behaviors that are sanctioned under criminal code and defined as illegal.

ENTERTAINMENT LAW

Entertainment law predominantly relates to Intellectual Property Law, but is more specifically centered on rights and royalties issues to media in the arts, music, television, cinema, or athletics.
